Estimate your user stories using the scrum poker technique even with remote teams!

Typical workflow:
  • The organizer invites team players to a group
  • The team players enter the group using their own devices
  • The organizer provides a short overview of the user story and starts the game
  • Each team player estimates the user story by selecting a card from the Fibonacci sequence
  • The results are updated in realtime on all devices
  • When the organizer decides that the time is up, he presses "View Results" and the estimates from all palyers are displayed on all devices
  • People with high estimates and low estimates justify their estimates and then the discussion continues
